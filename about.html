About Us

Objective

Quality Baker’s focuses on meeting all the dire requirements of a passionate baker. With almost 150+ curate products - from baking ingredients to tools, accessories, cake tins, pans, pattern nozzles, cupcake liners, designer muffin cups, variety of silicone moulds, edible decorative, few kitsch and quirky baking stuff - we've tried to show all the possible essentials you may need while baking. We trust that we will be your go-to one stop shop, providing an easy access to a range of interesting bake ware and edibles that fits your bill. We are in constant lookout for your baking requirements and ensure that we get our stock updated accordingly.

We Have

Our product section includes Cake Molds, Double Strawberry Molds, Rectangle Muffin Baking Trays, Non-Stick Silicone Cake Pans, Silicone Muffin Forms and Round Bottom Cake Molds. Apart from this, we offer Rose Cake Molds, Heart Shape Bread Making Molds, Cute Silicone Bear Bake Molds, Round Shaped Cake Molds, Star Form Cups and Heart Shaped Cake Decorations and many more.

Authentic

These products are made from 100% food-grade plastic, which is sourced from the authentic vendors of the market. The offered products are available in different designs, patterns and colours that are suitable for making cakes, muffins, chocolates and breads. Moreover, all our offered products are appreciated for their non-toxicity, ability to withstand adverse temperature conditions and odourless properties.
